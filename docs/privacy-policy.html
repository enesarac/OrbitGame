1. Kapsam

Bu politika, Android cihazlarda kullanilan Orbit oyunu icin gecerlidir. Orbit; hesap olusturma gerektirmeyen, tek oyunculu bir mobil arcade oyunudur. Oyunda skor, coin, magaza esyalari ve kozmetik secimler gibi ilerleme bilgileri bulunur.

2. Gelistiricinin Dogrudan Toplamadigi Veriler

Orbit, kullanicidan dogrudan ad, soyad, telefon numarasi, e-posta adresi, tam adres, kisi listesi, kamera, mikrofon, fotograf, dosya, hassas saglik bilgisi veya finansal bilgi istemez. Uygulamada kullanici hesabi veya giris sistemi yoktur.

3. Cihazda Yerel Olarak Saklanan Veriler

Orbit, oyun deneyimini surdurmek icin bazi bilgileri cihazda yerel olarak saklar:

  • Toplam coin miktari
  • En yuksek skor
  • Secili oyuncu rengi
  • Secili arka plan temasi
  • Secili iz efekti ve gunes gorunumu
  • Acik olan magaza esyalari
  • Enerji kalkani gibi oyun ici tek kullanimlik durumlar

Bu bilgiler cihazdaki uygulama depolamasinda tutulur ve Orbit tarafindan herhangi bir gelistirici sunucusuna gonderilmez. Kullanici uygulamayi kaldirarak veya Android ayarlarindan uygulama verilerini temizleyerek bu yerel verileri silebilir.

4. Ucuncu Taraf Servisler

Orbit; reklam, analiz ve hata raporlama amaclariyla Google servislerini kullanir.

Google AdMob: Orbit icinde odullu reklamlar ve oyun sonu reklamlar kullanilabilir. AdMob; reklam gosterimi, reklam olcumu, sahteciligi onleme, reklam performansi ve reklam kisilestirme/olcumleme amaclariyla IP adresi, reklam kimligi, cihaz veya uygulama tanimlayicilari, reklam etkilesimleri, uygulama etkilesimleri ve tanilama bilgileri gibi verileri isleyebilir.

Firebase Analytics: Orbit, oyunun dengesini ve kullanici deneyimini iyilestirmek icin anonim/istatistiksel oyun olaylarini kaydedebilir. Ornek olaylar: oyun baslangici, oyun bitisi, skor esikleri, coin toplama, odullu reklam kullanimi ve magaza esyasi secimi.

Firebase Crashlytics: Uygulama cokmesi veya teknik hata olustugunda hata raporlari toplanabilir. Bu raporlar; cihaz modeli, isletim sistemi surumu, uygulama surumu, hata ani teknik bilgileri ve cokme izleri gibi tanilama verilerini icerebilir.

This Privacy Policy explains what data may be processed in the Orbit mobile game, how advertising and analytics services are used, and what choices users have regarding their data.

App: Orbit
Package name: com.enesarac.orbit
Developer: Enes Arac
Effective date: July 11, 2026

1. Scope

This policy applies to the Orbit game on Android devices. Orbit is a single-player mobile arcade game that does not require account registration. The game includes progress-related information such as score, coins, shop items, and cosmetic selections.

2. Data Not Directly Collected by the Developer

Orbit does not directly ask users for their name, phone number, email address, full address, contacts, camera, microphone, photos, files, sensitive health information, or financial information. The app does not include a user account or login system.

3. Data Stored Locally on the Device

Orbit stores certain information locally on the device to maintain the game experience:

  • Total coin balance
  • High score
  • Selected player color
  • Selected background theme
  • Selected trail effect and sun skin
  • Unlocked shop items
  • One-time gameplay states such as the energy shield

This information is stored in the app storage on the device and is not sent by Orbit to a developer-operated server. Users can delete this local data by uninstalling the app or clearing the app data from Android settings.

4. Third-Party Services

Orbit uses Google services for advertising, analytics, and crash reporting.

Google AdMob: Orbit may show rewarded ads and game-over ads. AdMob may process data such as IP address, advertising ID, device or app identifiers, ad interactions, app interactions, and diagnostic information for ad serving, measurement, fraud prevention, performance, and personalization/analytics purposes.

Firebase Analytics: Orbit may log anonymous or statistical gameplay events to improve game balance and user experience. Example events include game start, game over, score milestones, coin collection, rewarded ad usage, and shop item selection.

Firebase Crashlytics: If the app crashes or encounters a technical error, crash reports may be collected. These reports may include device model, operating system version, app version, crash-time technical information, and stack traces.

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Google AdMob Policy
  • Firebase Privacy and Security Information

5. Ads and Rewards

Orbit may include rewarded ads. If a user chooses to watch a rewarded ad, they may receive in-game coins or a continue/revive reward. Interstitial ads may be shown after gameplay ends. If an ad is not ready, the related ad button may be temporarily unavailable.

6. Purposes of Data Processing

  • To operate the game and preserve local progress
  • To provide ads and rewarded ad features
  • To improve game balance, performance, and user experience
  • To detect and fix crashes and technical issues
  • To reduce fraud, abuse, and security risks

7. Data Sharing

The developer does not directly sell user data. However, third-party SDKs used in Orbit, such as Google AdMob, Firebase Analytics, and Firebase Crashlytics, may process or share data according to their own policies for advertising, analytics, crash reporting, security, and fraud prevention purposes.

8. Children's Privacy

Orbit is a general-audience arcade game and is not specifically directed to children. The developer does not knowingly collect personal information directly from children. If a parent or guardian has concerns about a child's app usage, they may contact the developer using the contact information below.

9. Security

Local game data is stored inside Android app storage. Data processed through Google services is subject to the security and transmission protections of the relevant Google services.

10. Data Retention and Deletion

Local game data may remain on the device until the user uninstalls the app or clears the app data. Advertising, analytics, and crash reporting data processed by Google services is subject to the applicable Google/Firebase settings and retention periods.

  • Clear Orbit app data from Android settings
  • Uninstall Orbit from the device
  • Reset or delete the Android advertising ID
  • Manage Google account and ad privacy settings

11. International Data Transfers

Data processed by Google services may be processed on servers outside the user's country. These operations are subject to Google's privacy policy, terms, and data processing conditions.

12. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. If important changes are made, the effective date on this page will be updated.
